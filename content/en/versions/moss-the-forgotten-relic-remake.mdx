<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Beginner Guide: Mastering Dual Controls">

Starting your journey in the **moss the forgotten relic remake** requires a shift in mindset. You are not just playing as the mouse; you are a giant spectral presence that exists within the world. Success depends on how well you coordinate these two distinct roles.

**Video Highlights:**
- Basic movement and jumping mechanics for Quill.
- How the Reader interacts with glowing objects.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Start with Book I">
    While you can jump straight into Book II, it is highly recommended to start with Book I. This serves as the tutorial for the game's "puzzle language," teaching you how to identify climbable ledges and interactive mechanisms.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify Reader Objects">
    Objects that the Reader can manipulate will have a faint, magical glow. Use your cursor to grab these blocks, statues, or gates. If a platform resets automatically, you must keep holding the interaction button while Quill crosses.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Read the Visual Cues">
    Look for white scratch marks on ledges and walls. These indicate where Quill can grab and climb. If a ledge is too high, there is almost certainly a block nearby that the Reader can move into position.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Manage Combat and Healing">
    Quill has a limited health pool. When she is panting or the screen edges turn red, use the Reader's cursor to "grab" Quill. This initiates a healing glow that restores her health over a few seconds.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Environmental Awareness">
Always rotate the camera or look behind foreground scenery. The "Smart Follow Camera" in the remake is excellent, but some scrolls are intentionally tucked behind pillars or inside breakable crates that are easy to miss.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Weapons, Abilities, and Combat Strategy">

Combat in the **moss the forgotten relic remake** is a dance between Quill's agility and the Reader's crowd control. While Quill uses her sword to deal damage, the Reader can grab enemies to stun them or even force them to fire at their own allies.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="The Sword">

  </Card>
  <Card title="The Chakram">
    A ranged weapon obtained in Book II. It can be charged by the Reader to strike multiple distant switches or enemies in a single throw.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cariel's Hammer">
    A heavy weapon for smashing armored "Forged" enemies. The **Hammer Trap** ability lets the Reader place a magical zone that crushes anything entering it.
  </Card>

<Callout type="warning" title="Enemy Manipulation">
Do not kill every enemy on sight. Many puzzles require you to keep a "Bomber" or "Screecher" alive so the Reader can drag them onto a pressure plate or use their projectiles to hit a distant switch.
</Callout>

For players who find the combat frustrating or simply want to enjoy the narrative, the remake includes a **Skip Combat** prompt. This appears during major encounters and allows you to bypass the fight while keeping the story moving. However, you will still need to use enemies for environmental puzzles.

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Environmental Puzzle Solutions">

Puzzles are the heart of the **moss the forgotten relic remake**. Most obstacles require a "work backward" logic: identify the exit, see what is blocking it, and trace the power line or path back to a movable object.

| Puzzle Type | Required Interaction | Pro Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Pressure Plates** | Move block or enemy | Use Reader to hold enemies in place |
| **Rotating Bridges** | Reader drag / rotate | Move Quill onto bridge *before* rotating |
| **Nature Vines** | Nature Attunement | Look for dormant plants near ledges |
| **Mirror Beams** | Light reflection | Align mirrors from the source outward |
| **Glass Platforms** | Reveal ability | Look for magical distortion in the air |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Analyze the Diorama">
    Before moving Quill, scan the entire room. Identify all interactive objects (glowing blue or green) and look for the exit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Clear the Path">
    Use the Reader to move any blocks or statues that are obstructing the floor level. This gives Quill more room to dodge if enemies spawn.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute the Sequence">
    Many puzzles are timed. You might need to pull a lever with Quill, then quickly grab a platform with the Reader to move her across before the gate closes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Nature Attunement">
In Book II, the Nature Attunement power allows you to grow vines. This is essential for backtracking to Book I areas via Chapter Select to find previously unreachable scrolls.
</Callout>

</Section>
