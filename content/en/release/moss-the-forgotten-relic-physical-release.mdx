Follow these steps to master the dual-control system and ensure Quill survives her journey through the forgotten ruins.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Synchronize Movement">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify Interactive Objects">
    Look for a soft blue or green glow on objects. These can be grabbed, pulled, or rotated by the Reader to clear paths for Quill.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Master the Heal Mechanic">
    When Quill takes damage, her glow will dim. Hover the Reader cursor over her and hold the interaction button to restore her health. Do this in safe corners!

  <Step num="4" title="Use the Skip Combat Feature">

  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Environmental Cues">

**Boss Encounter: Sarffog (Book I)**
The giant serpent Sarffog is a test of your speed and environmental awareness. As Quill climbs the ruins, you must use the Reader to move platforms quickly before Sarffog's fire breath reaches her. Focus on reaching the next checkpoint rather than trying to attack the serpent directly until the final phase.

**Boss Encounter: The Warden (Book II)**
This foundry-based boss requires the use of Cariel's Hammer. You must time your Hammer Traps (charged by the Reader) to hit the Warden's glowing weak points when they slam onto the arena floor.

<Callout type="warning" title="Watch the Markers">
Bosses often telegraph their attacks with orange or red floor markers. Ensure Quill is out of these zones before the impact, as late-game bosses can defeat her in just two hits.
</Callout>
