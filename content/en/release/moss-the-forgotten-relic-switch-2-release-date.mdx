<Section icon={Shield} title="Beginner Guide: Managing Quill and the Reader">

Succeeding in this fantasy world requires mastering the unique dual-character control system. Unlike traditional action-adventure titles where you only control the hero, you simultaneously guide Quill, the brave mouse protagonist, and the Reader, an ethereal giant force interacting with the physical environment.

Redesigned from the ground up for standard gamepads, the non-VR controls map Quill's physical actions to the left side of your controller, while the Reader's magical interactions are handled on the right. You must learn to coordinate both characters at once to solve environmental puzzles and survive intense Arcane encounters.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Start with Book I">
    While the main menu allows you to jump straight into Book II, starting with Book I is highly recommended. It introduces the core movement mechanics, slowly builds the puzzle vocabulary, and establishes the emotional bond between Quill and the Reader.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Separate Your Mental Controls">
    Train yourself to split your inputs. Use the left analog stick and face buttons to run, jump, climb, and swing Quill's sword. Use the right analog stick to guide the Reader's magical cursor, and hold the right trigger to grab physical objects.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Scan the Environment First">
    Before moving Quill into a new room, look for scratch marks on ledges, walls, and pillars. These visual indicators highlight climbable surfaces and guide you on where to position blocks or platforms.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Prepare the Path Ahead">
    Move heavy blocks, align floating platforms, and pull ancient switches before sending Quill across dangerous gaps. Preparing the entire route in advance minimizes failed platforming attempts and unnecessary resets.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Character | Action Type | Key Controller Inputs | Puzzle Application |
|---|---|---|---|
| Quill | Traversal | Left analog stick, jump button | Running, climbing, jumping ledges |
| Quill | Combat | Attack button, dodge trigger | Sword combinations, rapid evasions |
| Reader | Interaction | Right stick cursor, grab trigger | Dragging blocks, rotating platforms |
| Reader | Support | Highlighted grab, healing hold | Restraining enemies, healing Quill |

<Callout type="tip" title="Healing Strategy">
You can heal Quill at any time by guiding the Reader's cursor over her and holding the interaction trigger. To do this safely during combat, use the Reader to hold a nearby enemy or move Quill to a safe corner before initiating the heal.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics and Boss Strategies">

Combat is fast, fluid, and tactical. Success relies heavily on dodging enemy strikes, identifying attack patterns, and coordinating with the Reader to manipulate the battlefield. In Book II, Quill's arsenal expands beyond her trusty sword, introducing the ranged Chakram and the heavy Cariel's Hammer.

Each weapon serves a dual purpose. The sword is ideal for fast close-range combat and crossing large gaps via the Sword Dash. The Chakram allows you to strike distant targets and trigger remote switches. The Hammer deals massive damage to armored foes and activates heavy physical mechanisms.
