<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Strategy: Sword vs. Hammer">

The **moss the forgotten relic demo** introduces two distinct playstyles through its weaponry. You begin with a standard sword, which is excellent for mobility, but the real star of the demo is the Hammer found in the later stages.

| Feature | The Relic Sword | The Hammer of Caryll |

### Mastering the Hammer Mechanic
The Hammer isn't just a combat tool; it's a puzzle-solving device. When you acquire it, you'll notice specific "Hammer Symbols" on the floor or on heavy mechanisms.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Hammer Symbol">
    Find the circular glowing plate on the ground or a specific pillar.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Charge the Weapon">
    Hold the special attack button until the Hammer glows with arcane energy.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Strike the Target">
    Release the strike on the symbol to trigger a lasting effect, such as a platform rising or a gate opening.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Time the Trap">
    Some switches require you to strike them and then quickly move Quill to the next area before the charge dissipates.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Combat Synergy">
Use the Reader to grab an enemy and hold them in place, then switch to Quill and use a charged Hammer strike to deal massive AoE damage to surrounding foes.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Walkthrough: The Greenhouse and Beyond">

The demo's primary setting is a lush, overgrown greenhouse filled with wild plant life and ancient ruins. Progression is linear but rewards players who explore the verticality of each room.

### Key Locations in the Demo
1.  **The Throne Room Entrance:** This is where you meet Uncle Argus. You must pull a large stone door open using the Reader's grab mechanic.
2.  **The Sunken Garden:** A combat arena where you first encounter the Arcane. Use the dodge roll frequently here as the enemies attack in groups of three.
3.  **The Hammer Vault:** A hidden chamber beneath the greenhouse heart. You must solve a mind-control puzzle where an enemy is forced to stand on a pressure plate while Quill jumps across.
4.  **The Warden's Overlook:** The final area of the demo where you face the Cinder Knight.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Boss Encounter: The Cinder Knight Warden">

The **moss the forgotten relic demo** concludes with a dramatic confrontation against the Cinder Knight. This boss serves as a skill check for everything you've learned regarding movement and weapon timing.

| Phase | Boss Attack | Counter Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Phase 1** | Overhead Ground Slam | Roll to the side and strike the hand |
| **Phase 1** | Sweeping Blade | Jump over the blade as it passes |
| **Phase 2** | Arcane Projectiles | Use the Reader to block projectiles with objects |
| **Phase 2** | Hammer Vulnerability | Charge your Hammer and hit the glowing blue weak point |

During this fight, the Cinder Knight will occasionally rest his massive hand on the platform. This is your window to deal damage. If you have the Hammer equipped, a charged strike to his knuckles will stun him, allowing Quill to land a full combo on his face.

<Callout type="warning" title="Environmental Hazards">
The arena floor will occasionally glow red. This indicates an incoming AoE blast. Move Quill to the outer edges of the platform to avoid massive damage.
</Callout>
