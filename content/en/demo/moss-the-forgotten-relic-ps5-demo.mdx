<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Walkthrough of the Demo">

Navigating the demo requires a blend of platforming precision and environmental puzzle-solving. This step-by-step walkthrough details how to progress through each major chamber and solve the environmental riddles.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Throne Room Escape">
    Begin by meeting Uncle Argus in the ruined throne room. He warns you of Sarbog's looming presence. To open the main stone gate, control the Reader's spectral hands to grab the golden door handle and pull it firmly to the left. Guide Quill through the opening while keeping an eye out for breakable pots containing health and supplies.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Greenhouse Ascent">
    Upon reaching the greenhouse, climb the stone ledges and clear the wild overgrowth with Quill's sword. You will encounter a moving platform. As the Reader, grab the platform and slide it back and forth to bridge the gap. Time Quill's jump carefully to avoid falling into the abyss below.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Acquiring the Hammer of Caryll">
    In the central chamber, interact with the pedestal to claim the Hammer of Caryll. To proceed, you must activate the hammer trap. Use Quill to strike the glowing blue crystal with a heavy hammer attack, charging the mechanism. Then, as the Reader, grab the charged energy and transfer it to the overhead crane to lift the heavy blocking gate.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Warden Confrontation">
    The demo culminates in an encounter with the Cinder Knight, a massive robotic warden. Dodge the Warden's sweeping hand attacks. When the Warden slams its hand onto the platform, quickly charge Quill's hammer and strike the glowing weak point on its wrist to disable its shield and end the demo.
  </Step>
</Steps>
