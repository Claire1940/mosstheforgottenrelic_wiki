<Section icon={Target} title="Boss Guide: Defeating the Cinder Knight">

The climax of the demo pits Quill against the formidable Cinder Knight, a massive armored warden who previously decimated Uncle Argus's battalion. This encounter tests your ability to dodge heavy attacks and strike during narrow windows of vulnerability.

| Boss Phase | Danger Level | Boss Attack Pattern | Counter Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Phase 1 | Moderate | Single-arm sweeping strikes across the platform | Double jump over the sweeping arm, then execute quick sword strikes against the exposed hand. |
| Phase 2 | High | Overhead fist slams that cause shockwaves | Time your dodge roll to bypass the shockwave, then immediately switch to the hammer to strike the glowing wrist node. |
| Phase 3 | Extreme | Combined sweeps and continuous ground slams | Stay mobile, avoid the center of the arena, and use fully charged hammer strikes when the boss rests after a slam. |

The key to this battle is patience. Do not try to rush your attacks, as the Cinder Knight's recovery animations are brief.

<Callout type="tip" title="Hammer Charge Timing">
Only attempt fully charged hammer strikes when the Warden's hand is completely stationary on the ground after a heavy slam. Trying to charge the hammer during his sweeping phases will leave you completely exposed to high-damage attacks.
</Callout>
