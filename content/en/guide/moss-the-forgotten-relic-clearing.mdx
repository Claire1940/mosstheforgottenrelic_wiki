<Section icon={Target} title="Moss the Forgotten Relic Clearing: Step-by-Step Guide">

The "Clearing" is the introductory chapter of Book I, where you first bond with Quill. This section establishes the fundamental mechanics of **moss the forgotten relic clearing** and sets the pace for the rest of the adventure.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Initial Meeting">
    After the opening narration, you find Quill in a sun-drenched clearing. Use the left stick to move her toward the glowing relic. As the Reader, reach out and interact with the relic to trigger the bond.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigating the Forest Path">
    Guide Quill through the first few screens. You will encounter a high ledge that is unreachable. Locate the large stone block nearby, grab it with the Reader cursor, and slide it against the ledge to create a step for Quill.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Solving the Water Gate Puzzle">
    You will reach a small stream blocked by a gate. Move Quill to the pressure plate on the left. While she stands there, use the Reader to rotate the overhead mechanism until the gate locks into the open position.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="First Combat Encounter">
    Small beetle-like Arcane enemies will spawn. Use Quill's sword to dispatch them. Practice the dodge move (roll) to avoid their lunging attacks. If Quill takes damage, hold her with the Reader to heal.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Exiting the Clearing">
    Climb the final vine-covered wall. Before leaving the area, look behind the large tree in the background to find your first hidden scroll fragment. Once collected, proceed through the archway to continue the journey.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Exploration Reward">

</Callout>
