<Section icon={Shield} title="Final Encounter: Cinder Knight Warden Strategy">

The demo culminates in a confrontation with the **Cinder Knight Warden**. This massive boss represents the "barbaric war-obsessed" guardians mentioned by Uncle Argus. The fight is less about direct sword combat and more about using the Hammer of Caryll effectively.

**Boss Phases and Tactics:**
1. **The Hand Slam**: The Warden will attempt to crush Quill with his massive gauntlet. Dodge the initial impact.
2. **The Vulnerable State**: After a slam, the Warden's hand remains on the ground for a few seconds.
3. **Hammer Charge**: Switch to the Hammer of Caryll and perform a fully charged strike on the Warden's knuckles.
4. **Environment Interaction**: Use the Reader to pull down debris from the ceiling when the Warden roars, stunning him for a longer duration.

| Boss Attack | Warning Sign | How to Avoid |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Overhead Slam** | Warden raises hand high | Roll to the side immediately |
| **Cinder Sweep** | Fire appears on the floor | Jump over the shockwave |
| **Arcane Roar** | Screen shakes violently | Stand behind stone pillars |

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Boss Tip">
Do not try to attack the Warden's body directly with the sword. It does zero damage. You must wait for the hand slam and use the Hammer of Caryll to deal meaningful damage.
</Callout>
