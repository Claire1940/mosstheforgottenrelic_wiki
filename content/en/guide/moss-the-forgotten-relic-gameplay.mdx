<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering the Hammer Puzzle">

One of the standout features of the **moss the forgotten relic gameplay** demo is the Hammer of Caryll puzzle. This requires the player to synchronize Quill's movements with the Reader's ability to manipulate the environment.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Hammer Altar">
    Find the glowing pedestal containing the Hammer of Caryll. Once Quill picks it up, her movement becomes slightly more weighted.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Charge the Weapon">
    Look for specific hammer symbols on the floor or walls. Strike these points to charge the hammer with energy.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Trigger the Trap">
    Move Quill to the platform near the heavy trap mechanism. Use the charged hammer to strike the activation switch.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Mind Control Synergy">
    If a gate remains closed, mind-control a nearby Arcane unit and position it on the pressure plate while Quill crosses the gap.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Puzzle Strategy">
