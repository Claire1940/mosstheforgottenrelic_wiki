<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics and Input Mapping">

A significant change in the **moss the forgotten relic game engine** is the adaptation of VR interactions to standard controllers. The engine now uses a "Virtual Hand" system mapped to the right analog stick or mouse, allowing players to reach into the world and manipulate objects while simultaneously controlling Quill with the left stick. This hybrid control scheme is essential for the new combat encounters introduced in this version.

The introduction of the **Hammer of Cariel** adds a new layer of physics-based combat. The engine calculates the weight and momentum of the hammer, meaning swings have a wind-up time and a recovery phase. Players must master the "Posture Bar" system, where successive hits or successful parries can break an enemy's guard, leading to a massive damage critical strike.
