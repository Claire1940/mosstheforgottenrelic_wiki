<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Step-by-Step Mire Temple Walkthrough">

To successfully clear the **moss the forgotten relic mire temple**, follow these sequential steps. This walkthrough covers the path from the entrance to the final chamber of Chapter 3, ensuring you don't miss the critical progression points.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Entrance Gate">
    Upon arriving at the temple exterior, you will see a large locked gate. Look to the left for a movable stone block. As the Reader, drag the block to the right side of the wall. Switch to Quill and climb the block to reach the upper lever. Pulling this lever opens the main entrance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The First Mire Room">
    The floor is flooded. Move Quill to the first stationary platform. Use the Reader to rotate the central bridge toward Quill. Walk across, then rotate the bridge again while Quill is standing on it to reach the far exit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Arcane Manipulation">
    You will encounter a pressure plate that opens the exit, but it resets when Quill steps off. Locate the beetle-like Arcane enemy in the room. Use the Reader to grab and hold the enemy, dragging it directly onto the plate. This keeps the door open, allowing Quill to pass.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Twilight Portal">
    Before leaving the central hub, check the side of the large gate. You will see a shimmering purple effect. This is the first **Twilight Garden portal**. Entering this is optional but recommended for obtaining upgraded equipment that helps with later Book II puzzles.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Vertical Ascent">
    In the final room, you must raise a series of three lifts. Move Quill to the first lift, raise it with the Reader, and jump to the second. Repeat this for the third lift, but be careful of the ranged Ticker enemies firing from the balconies.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Checkpoint Reached">
The game uses a **regular autosave system**. If Quill falls during the vertical ascent, you will restart at the beginning of that specific room rather than the start of the temple.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Strategies for Chapter 3">

Combat in the **moss the forgotten relic mire temple** introduces the need for Reader-assisted crowd control. You will face "Screechers" (melee beetles) and "Tickers" (ranged explosive units). In the narrow corridors of the temple, getting surrounded is the primary threat.

The "Skip Combat" feature is available for those who prefer focusing on puzzles, but for those engaging in battle, the synergy between Quill's sword and the Reader's grip is essential. By holding a Screecher, the Reader prevents it from attacking, allowing Quill to focus on other threats or deal massive damage to the restrained target.

| **Screecher** | Melee Swipe | Slow turn speed | Dodge behind and strike |
| **Ticker** | Ranged Projectile | Low Health | Use Reader to redirect shots |

| Combat Ability | Unlock Status | Combat Utility |

| **Sword Combo** | Default | Basic damage against beetles |
| **Dodge Roll** | Default | Essential for avoiding Ticker blasts |
| **Reader Heal** | Default | Hold Quill to restore health |

<Callout type="info" title="Healing Quill">
If Quill's health is low (indicated by the screen turning greyish), use the Reader to **reach out and touch her**. Holding the interaction button will pulse green energy, healing her back to full health. Do this in a safe corner away from enemies.
</Callout>
