<Section icon={Target} title="Environmental Puzzle Solutions">

Most environmental obstacles follow a simple rule: Quill handles the traversal while the Reader changes the room layout. When you find yourself stuck, look at the final exit door and work backward to identify the blocks, switches, or enemies required to clear the path.

| Puzzle Type | Reader Interaction | Quill Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Movable Blocks | Drag blocks to scratched ledges | Climb to reach higher platforms |
| Rotating Statues | Rotate gear mechanisms | Cross before the platform resets |
| Pressure Plates | Grab and hold Arcane enemies | Stand on the plate or run through gates |
| Glowing Switches | Strike switches with sword | Time the dash through moving barriers |

For puzzles involving pressure plates, do not defeat every enemy immediately. Stun and drag a beetle-like Arcane enemy onto the plate to keep gates open, allowing Quill to pass through to the next section.

<Callout type="info" title="Skip Combat Feature">
If a combat arena or boss encounter becomes too difficult, open the pause menu to use the Skip Combat option. This allows you to bypass the battle while keeping the environmental puzzle elements intact.
</Callout>
