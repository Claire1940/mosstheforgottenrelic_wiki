<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step: Mastering the Non-VR Controls">

Transitioning from VR to a standard controller changes how you interact with the world. Follow these steps to optimize your playstyle for the best experience.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Configure Your Cursor">
    The Reader interaction is now mapped to the right analog stick (controller) or mouse. Adjust your sensitivity in the settings to ensure you can grab blocks and heal Quill quickly during frantic battles.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify Interaction Points">
    Look for the "glowing" highlights. Objects that the Reader can move will have a distinct white or blue aura. On standard screens, these are much easier to spot than in the original VR version.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Master the 'Hold and Move' Technique">
    Practice holding a platform in place with the Reader while simultaneously navigating Quill across it. This is the foundation of almost every advanced puzzle in Book II.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Utilize Nature Attunement">
    Once you unlock Nature Attunement in the Sprite Realm, remember that you can grow vines to create new climbing paths. This is key for finding hidden collectible scrolls.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Platform Awareness">
Unlike the VR version where you could lean in to see around corners, you must now rely on the **Smart Follow Camera**. If a path seems blocked, try moving Quill to the edge of the screen to trigger a camera shift.
</Callout>

</Section>
