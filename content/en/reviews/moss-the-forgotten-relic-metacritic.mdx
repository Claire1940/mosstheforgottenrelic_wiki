<Section icon={Map} title="Beginner Guide: Mastering Quill and the Reader">

In this unique adventure, you do not just play as the hero; you play as her giant companion as well. You simultaneously control Quill, a brave young mouse seeking to save her kingdom, and the Reader, an ethereal presence that can reach into the world to move heavy objects, heal Quill, and manipulate enemies. Mastering this dual-control scheme is the key to solving the game's intricate environmental puzzles and surviving dangerous combat encounters.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Start with Book I">
    While the main menu allows you to jump straight into Book II, starting with Book I is highly recommended. It introduces the mechanics, characters, and puzzle language at a comfortable pace.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Separate Your Inputs">
    Train your hands to work independently. Use your left analog stick (or WASD keys) to guide Quill's movement, and use the right stick (or mouse cursor) to direct the Reader's magical interactions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Analyze the Diorama">
    Every room is a self-contained puzzle box. Before moving Quill, scan the environment for scratch marks on ledges, which indicate climbable paths, and highlightable objects that the Reader can grab.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Use Enemies as Tools">
    Do not defeat every beetle-like Arcane enemy immediately. Many puzzles require you to use the Reader to grab an enemy and hold them down on a pressure plate to keep a gate open for Quill.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Heal in Safe Zones">
    During hectic battles, Quill will take damage. Move her to a safe corner or behind cover, then use the Reader's cursor to hold and heal her before jumping back into the fray.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Accessibility Feature">
If you find the combat sections too difficult or prefer to focus entirely on the story and puzzles, you can activate the optional **Skip Combat** feature from the menu at any time.
</Callout>

</Section>
