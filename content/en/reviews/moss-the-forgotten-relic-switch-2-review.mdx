<Section icon={Trophy} title="Collectibles and End-Game Goals">

For completionists, the game offers a wealth of hidden secrets spread across its dioramas. Collecting these items is essential for unlocking the full stained-glass artwork and obtaining the most powerful armor sets.

- **Forgotten Fragments (Scrolls)**: There are 31 in Book I and 48 in Book II. They are often hidden behind foreground scenery or on high ledges that require creative block placement.
- **Relic Dust**: Obtained by breaking pots, barrels, and crates. This dust is used to open **Relic Shrines** containing secret armor.
- **Twilight Portals**: Three hidden entrances in Book I that lead to the Twilight Garden expansion content.

| Collectible Type | Total Count | Reward |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Book I Scrolls** | 31 Fragments | Stained-Glass Art |
| **Book II Scrolls** | 48 Fragments | Lore & Completion |
| **Relic Shrines** | 3 Locations | Secret Armor Sets |
| **Twilight Portals** | 3 Portals | Upgraded Equipment |

<Callout type="tip" title="Backtracking">
Many collectibles in Book I can only be reached after obtaining the **Nature Attunement** or **Sword Dash** abilities in Book II. Don't worry if you can't find everything on your first pass.
</Callout>
