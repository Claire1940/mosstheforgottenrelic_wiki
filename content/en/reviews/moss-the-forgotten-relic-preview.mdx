<Section icon={Target} title="Dual-Control Mechanics and Beginner Guide">

The core innovation of this adaptation lies in its dual-control system. Instead of relying on VR motion controllers, players manage two distinct roles simultaneously using a standard gamepad or a keyboard-and-mouse setup. You control Quill, the brave mouse protagonist, with direct movement and combat inputs, while simultaneously operating the Reader—a magical, floating presence that interacts with the environment, manipulates heavy obstacles, and heals Quill during battle.

To succeed in this fantasy world, beginners must learn to read each diorama-like room before moving forward. Look for scratch marks on ledges, walls, and movable surfaces, which indicate where Quill can climb or where the Reader must position blocks.

| Action | Controller Mapping | Keyboard & Mouse Mapping | Interaction Type |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Quill Movement** | Left Analog Stick | WASD Keys | Direct Character Control |
| **Quill Combat** | Right Trigger / Face Buttons | Left Mouse Click / Space | Melee Swings & Dodges |
| **Reader Grab** | Left/Right Grip | Right Mouse Click & Drag | Environmental Interaction |
| **Reader Heal** | Hover & Hold Button | Hover & Hold Key | Magical Support |

<Section icon={Shield} title="Boss Battles and Combat Systems">

Combat in this adventure is tactical and rewards careful positioning over button-mashing. Standard encounters feature mixed groups of ranged, melee, and explosive Arcane forces. Boss battles elevate these mechanics, requiring players to coordinate Quill's evasion with active Reader intervention.

During major encounters, the Reader must manipulate the boss's weapons, trigger environmental traps, and manage smaller enemy waves to keep Quill safe.

| Boss Encounter | Main Attack Patterns | Recommended Strategy | Reader's Primary Role |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Sarffog (Book I)** | Fiery breath, path destruction, rapid strikes | Stay mobile, jump between platforms, strike during openings | Maintain escape routes, operate switches |
| **The Warden (Book II)** | Fist slams, shockwaves, heated hammer strikes | Dodge orange impact markers, jump over heat waves | Activate Hammer Traps, restrain boss weapons |
| **Tylan (Book II)** | Ground pounds, aerial dives, chase sequences | Prepare Hammer Traps, use Chakram on targets, pull bells | Charge Quill's weapons, manipulate arena objects |

<Callout type="warning" title="Skip Combat Option">
If you find a combat arena or boss encounter too difficult, the game includes an optional Skip Combat accessibility feature. Activating this prompt allows you to bypass the fight and continue enjoying the story.
</Callout>
