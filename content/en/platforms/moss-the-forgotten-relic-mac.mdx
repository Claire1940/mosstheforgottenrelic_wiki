<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Install Whisky or CrossOver">
    Download and install your preferred translation layer. Whisky is recommended for a free, user-friendly interface built on top of Apple's Game Porting Toolkit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Create a Windows Bottle">
    Create a new Windows 10 or Windows 11 bottle within the application. Ensure that DXVK and MSync or ESync are enabled in the bottle settings for optimal performance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Install Steam inside the Bottle">
    Download the Windows Steam installer (.exe) and run it through your translation layer. Log into your account and download the game client directly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Configure Launch Arguments">
    Right-click the game in Steam, select Properties, and add launch arguments like `-dx12` or `-windowed` if you encounter startup crashes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Shader Pre-Caching">
Enable Steam's shader pre-caching feature to reduce initial gameplay stuttering while assets load during the first few levels.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Performance Optimization & Graphics Settings">

Fine-tuning your graphical settings is essential to maintaining a high framerate during intense combat sequences and puzzle-solving segments. Apple Silicon GPUs handle texture rendering efficiently, but heavy post-processing effects can introduce performance bottlenecks.

To maximize your hardware efficiency, lower demanding settings such as dynamic shadows and volumetric effects. Utilizing resolution scaling or spatial upscaling technologies can also provide a significant boost to your overall framerate.

| Setting | Recommended Value | Performance Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Resolution** | 1920x1080 (Scaled) | High performance gain |
| **Shadow Quality** | Medium | Moderate performance gain |
| **Anti-Aliasing** | Temporal AA or FXAA | Low performance impact |
| **Texture Quality** | High (if 16GB RAM) | Low impact on modern chips |
| **V-Sync** | Enabled | Prevents screen tearing |
