<Section icon={Target} title="Beginner Guide: Mastering the Dual-Control System">

The core of **moss the forgotten relic vr** is the partnership between two distinct characters. Quill is the brave mouse protagonist you guide through the world, while the Reader (you) is a god-like presence that can manipulate the environment. Success depends on your ability to coordinate these two roles simultaneously.

<Callout type="tip" title="Interaction Tip">
The Reader's cursor can do more than move blocks. You can reach into the world to heal Quill by holding the interaction button over her, or even stun enemies to give her an opening.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initialize the Bond">
    Start with Book I. While Book II is accessible immediately, the first book establishes the mechanical language of the game and introduces the story in its intended sequence.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Learn Separate Inputs">
    Quill handles platforming (running, jumping, climbing) and melee combat. The Reader uses a cursor to grab blocks, rotate mechanisms, and pull heavy gates. Practice moving Quill while rotating a platform to master the flow.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Scan for Visual Cues">
    Look for white scratch marks on ledges and walls. These are universal indicators that Quill can climb that surface. If a ledge is too high, look for a nearby block the Reader can move into position.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Prepare the Route">
    Before moving Quill into a dangerous area, use the Reader to align platforms and activate switches. Building the route first prevents unnecessary resets and keeps the pacing smooth.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Combat Awareness">
Do not defeat every enemy immediately. Many puzzles require you to use the Reader to hold a beetle-like Arcane enemy on a pressure plate to keep a door open for Quill.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Puzzle Solutions and Environmental Mechanics">

Puzzles in **moss the forgotten relic vr** often function as "living dioramas." If you find yourself stuck, work backward from the exit. Identify what is blocking the path and determine which Reader ability can clear it.

| Puzzle Type | Required Mechanic | Common Solution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **High Ledge** | Reader Movement | Drag a block or statue beneath the scratched ledge. |
| **Rotating Bridge** | Reader Control | Hold the mechanism in place while Quill crosses. |
| **Pressure Plate** | Arcane Manipulation | Stun an enemy and drag them onto the switch. |
| **Glass Platform** | Reveal Power | Use the Reader's reveal ability to show hidden paths. |
| **Vine Bridge** | Nature Attunement | Grow ivy to connect two disconnected platforms. |
| **Heavy Gate** | Cariel's Hammer | Smash the armored lock with a charged hammer strike. |

<Callout type="tip" title="Observation is Key">
If a platform resets automatically when you let go, it means the Reader must continue holding it while you guide Quill across with the other hand.
</Callout>

</Section>
