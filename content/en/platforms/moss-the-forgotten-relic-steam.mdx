<Section icon={Target} title="Dual-Control System and Beginner Guide">

The core mechanic of this adventure revolves around the unique partnership between two distinct entities: Quill, a brave young mouse who handles platforming and melee combat, and the Reader, a giant magical presence controlled directly by the player. Succeeding in this world requires you to coordinate both characters simultaneously.

Quill is controlled using standard directional inputs for running, jumping, dodging, and attacking. Meanwhile, the Reader uses a mouse cursor or controller analog stick to grab heavy blocks, rotate ancient mechanisms, heal Quill during battle, and restrain hostile Arcane forces.

| Action | Keyboard & Mouse | Controller (Xbox / PS) | Gameplay Context |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Quill Move** | WASD | Left Analog Stick | Controls Quill's walking, running, and climbing |
| **Quill Jump** | Spacebar | A Button / Cross | Used for platforming and crossing short gaps |
| **Quill Attack** | Left Click | X Button / Square | Performs basic sword swings and melee combos |
| **Quill Dodge** | Shift | B Button / Circle | Evades enemy attacks and moves out of danger zones |
| **Reader Interact** | Right Click + Drag | Right Trigger + Analog | Moves blocks, heals Quill, and controls Arcane units |
