<Section icon={Target} title="Beginner Guide: Mastering Dual Controls">

To succeed in **moss the forgotten relic ps5**, you must learn to think as two characters at once. Quill handles the traditional platforming and combat, while you, the Reader, manipulate the world around her. On the PS5, these roles are mapped to the DualSense controller, allowing for fluid multitasking.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Understand the Reader Role">
    The Reader is represented by a glowing orb on the screen. Use the right analog stick to move this cursor and the trigger buttons to grab objects. You can pull blocks, rotate statues, and even hold enemies in place while Quill attacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Master Quill's Agility">
    Quill is fast and responsive. Use the left stick for movement and the face buttons for jumping and dodging. Unlike many platformers, Quill can climb ledges marked with visible white scratches—always look for these visual cues to find the intended path.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Environmental Synergy">
    Puzzles often require simultaneous input. For example, you might need to hold a heavy gate open with the Reader while guiding Quill through it. If you release the trigger, the gate will close, so maintain your grip until Quill is safely on the other side.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Healing and Support">
    Quill can take damage from enemies or environmental hazards. As the Reader, you can heal her by moving your cursor over her and holding the interaction button. Her heart will glow blue, indicating her health is being restored.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Success: Combat Synergy">
During combat, try grabbing a "Ticker" (beetle enemy) with the Reader and dragging it toward other enemies. When it explodes, it will damage the surrounding Arcane forces without harming Quill.
</Callout>

### Boss Encounter Tactics

When facing major bosses like **Sarffog** the serpent or **Tylan** the owl, the environment is your greatest weapon. You cannot simply hack and slash your way to victory.

1.  **Observe Patterns**: Bosses usually have three distinct phases. Watch for orange markers on the ground that indicate where an attack will land.
2.  **Reader Interference**: Use the Reader to pull levers or activate traps while the boss is recovering from a heavy attack.
3.  **Charged Abilities**: In Book II, charge Quill's weapon by holding your Reader cursor over her. A fully charged Hammer or Chakram is often required to hit boss weak points.

<Callout type="warning" title="Warning: Skip Combat">
If you find a specific battle room too difficult, the PS5 version includes a "Skip Combat" prompt in the pause menu. Use this to bypass the fight and continue the story, but remember that you will still need to solve the room's environmental puzzles manually.
</Callout>
