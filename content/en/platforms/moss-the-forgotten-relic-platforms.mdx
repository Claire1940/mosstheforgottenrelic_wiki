<Section icon={Target} title="Control Schemes and Dual-Character Mechanics">

The core gameplay centers on a unique dual-control system. You simultaneously control Quill, the brave mouse protagonist, and the Reader, an ethereal giant who can manipulate the physical environment. Adapting these mechanics to standard controllers required a thoughtful redesign of the input layout.

Standard gamepads split these responsibilities across the left and right sides of the controller. The left analog stick and face buttons manage Quill's movement, combat, and platforming. The right analog stick controls the Reader's cursor, allowing you to grab objects, heal Quill, and influence enemies.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Master Dual-Character Movement">
    Use the left analog stick to guide Quill through platforming hazards, while using the right analog stick to position the Reader's magical cursor over interactive elements.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Interact with Puzzle Objects">
    Hold the right trigger (R2/RT) when the Reader's cursor highlights a glowing object. You can drag heavy stone blocks, rotate pillars, and pull levers to clear a path for Quill.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Control Arcane Enemies">
    Stun and grab beetle-like Arcane enemies using the Reader's interaction. You can drag them onto pressure plates, force them to fire at distant switches, or hold them still while Quill attacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Heal Quill in Combat">
    When Quill takes damage, guide the Reader's cursor over her and hold the interaction button. A blue magical glow will restore her health, allowing her to jump back into the fight.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Control Coordination">
During intense combat rooms and boss battles, managing both characters simultaneously can feel overwhelming. Take a moment to analyze the room before engaging enemies. Identify safe zones where Quill can stand while you use the Reader to manipulate the environment or disable threats.
</Callout>

For players who find the real-time action too demanding, the game includes a highly praised accessibility feature: **Skip Combat**. This option allows you to bypass difficult battle arenas and boss phases entirely, letting you focus solely on the story, exploration, and environmental puzzles.

</Section>
