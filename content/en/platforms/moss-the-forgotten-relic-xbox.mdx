<Section icon={Gamepad2} title="Controller Mapping and Reader Mechanics">

Interacting with the world of **moss the forgotten relic xbox** requires a unique blend of standard platforming controls and "Reader" interactions. Unlike traditional third-person games, you are simultaneously controlling Quill's movements and your own spectral hands. The Xbox Wireless Controller has been mapped to provide tactile feedback through impulse triggers, making every environmental interaction feel substantial.

| Action | Xbox Input | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Move Quill** | Left Stick | Standard 360-degree movement for the hero. |
| **Move Reader Orb** | Right Stick | Controls the spectral cursor to interact with objects. |
| **Quill Jump** | A Button | Basic jump; can be combined with attacks. |
| **Quill Attack** | X Button | Fast sword swipes for combat encounters. |
| **Reader Grab** | RT (Right Trigger) | Interact with blocks, enemies, or heal Quill. |
| **Dodge/Roll** | B Button | Essential for avoiding heavy enemy strikes. |

Managing both characters simultaneously is the core challenge. As the Reader, you can reach into the world to pull heavy stone blocks, freeze enemies in place, or even manipulate the environment to create new paths for Quill. This dual-control system is what defines the "Forgotten Relic" experience.

<Callout type="tip" title="Combat Strategy">
You can use the Right Trigger to "grab" certain enemies. This freezes them temporarily, allowing Quill to safely strike them or move past difficult encounters without taking damage.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat and Boss Strategies">

Combat in **moss the forgotten relic xbox** is a dance between Quill's blade and the Reader's influence. You will face "The Forged," mechanical insects and warriors that guard the path to the relic. Success depends on your ability to multitask.

**Key Combat Tactics:**
- **The Pincer Move**: Use the Reader to grab an enemy from behind while Quill attacks from the front. This prevents the enemy from blocking.
- **Environmental Hazards**: Many arenas have traps. Use the Reader to trigger floor spikes or swinging logs when enemies pass over them.
- **Healing Priority**: Always keep an eye on Quill's glow. If her tunic starts to fade, hold the Reader orb over her immediately to prevent a restart.

| Enemy Type | Weakness | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Forged Ticker** | Back Plate | Dodge their lunge and strike the glowing core. |
| **Forged Scorcher** | Water Barrels | Use the Reader to tip water barrels onto them. |
| **Mechanical Warden** | Joint Hinges | Freeze their arms with RT, then have Quill strike the legs. |
